Overview

SALES FLOW NAVIGATION

The sales flow consists of 6 steps, each with specific scripts and tips:

  1. Opening - Build rapport and set expectations
  2. Discovery - Understand client needs and concerns
  3. Solution - Present tailored recommendations
  4. Benefits - Highlight key advantages
  5. Objections - Address client concerns
  6. Closing - Finalize the consultation
Navigation Tips

Use the Previous and Next buttons to navigate between steps. The progress bar at the top shows your current position in the flow.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely on Firebase servers with role-based access control.
Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Limited functionality is available offline, but internet connection is required for data synchronization.
Q: How often is the app updated?
A: Regular updates are released monthly with new features and improvements.
Best Practices
  • Always keep your app updated to the latest version
  • Regularly backup your client data
  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
