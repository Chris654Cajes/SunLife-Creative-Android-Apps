Step-by-Step User Guide

1

Launch the App

Open the SunLife Financial Planning app from your device. You'll see the main screen with the SunLife logo and two main options: "Add Survey" and "View Records".

2

Create a New Survey

Tap "Add Survey" to start a new financial planning questionnaire. Fill in all required personal information including name, email, and contact number. These fields are marked as mandatory and must be completed before proceeding.

3

Complete Family Information

Select your marital status from the available options (Single, Married, Single Parent). If you're married or a single parent, the number of children field will appear for you to specify how many kids you have.

4

Define Financial Goals

Choose your primary financial concerns from the list including managing money, saving for health, emergency funds, child education, or retirement. You can select multiple options. If you choose "Others", a text field will appear for you to specify additional goals.

5

Provide Financial Status

Answer questions about your current financial situation including whether you have existing investments, health insurance plans, knowledge about insurance products, and if you're open to free financial planning discussions.

6

Specify Savings Location

In the final section, describe where you currently keep your savings. This could be banks, investment accounts, or other financial instruments. Provide as much detail as possible for better financial planning recommendations.

7

Save Your Survey

Review all your answers and tap the "Save" button to submit your survey. The app will validate that all required fields are completed and save your response to the local database. You'll receive a confirmation message once the survey is successfully saved.

8

View and Manage Records

From the main screen, tap "View Records" to see all saved surveys. Use the filter options to search by name, email, marital status, or other criteria. Each record shows key information and quick action buttons for copying contact details or editing entries.

9

Export Data

Use the "Export CSV" button to download all survey data in a spreadsheet format. This is useful for analysis, reporting, or sharing with financial advisors. The exported file includes all survey responses in a structured format.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I edit a saved survey? expand_more
To edit a saved survey, go to the "View Records" screen, find the survey you want to modify, and tap the "Edit" button. This will open the survey form with pre-filled data that you can update and save again.
Is my financial data secure? expand_more
Yes, your data is stored locally on your device with proper security measures. The app only requests necessary permissions for file operations and doesn't transmit data to external servers without your explicit consent during export.
Can I export data to other formats besides CSV? expand_more
Currently, the app supports CSV export format which is widely compatible with spreadsheet applications like Excel, Google Sheets, and financial planning software. Future updates may include additional export formats.
What happens if I don't fill in all required fields? expand_more
The app will validate your input before saving. If any required fields (name, email, contact number) are missing, you'll see an error message highlighting which fields need to be completed. Optional fields can be left blank.
How many surveys can I store? expand_more
There's no strict limit on the number of surveys you can store. The app uses efficient local storage and can handle thousands of records. Performance may vary based on your device's available storage and memory.
Can I share individual survey results? expand_more
Yes, you can copy contact information directly from the records screen using the quick action buttons. For complete survey data, you'll need to export to CSV and share the file through your preferred method.
