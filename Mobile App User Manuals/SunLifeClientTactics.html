Main Interface - Clean and intuitive design for quick analysis

  1. Launch the App - Open SunLife Client Tactics from your device. The app features a clean, professional interface with the SunLife branding.
  2. Select Rejection Category - Choose from 15 comprehensive categories that best describe your client's objection. Categories range from "Lack of Trust" to "Price Concerns" to "External Influences."
  3. Identify Specific Reason - Once you select a category, choose the specific reason that matches your client's situation. Each category contains 3-5 detailed reasons.
  4. Get Tactical Suggestions - Tap the "Suggestions" button to receive 4-6 proven recovery strategies specifically designed for that rejection scenario.
  5. Apply the Strategies - Review the suggestions and implement the most appropriate tactics in your client interactions.

Example Scenarios

Scenario 1: Price Concern

Client says: "The premium is too expensive for me right now."

Category: Price / Affordability Concerns

Reason: Premium is too expensive

Suggested Tactics:

  • Offer flexible payment options (quarterly, semi-annual)
  • Present lower coverage alternatives
  • Break down cost into daily equivalents ($3/day vs $90/month)
  • Provide budgeting guidance and financial planning

Scenario 2: Trust Issues

Client says: "I need to think about it" (but seems hesitant)

Category: Hidden Objections

Reason: Client says they will think about it

Suggested Tactics:

  • Ask specifically what they want to think about
  • Clarify their concerns directly
  • Set a concrete follow-up schedule
  • Build more rapport before closing
