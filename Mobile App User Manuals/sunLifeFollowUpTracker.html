Understanding Status Levels

HOT

High Priority

Prospects showing strong interest or ready to close. Requires immediate attention and frequent follow-ups.

WARM

Medium Priority

Prospects showing some interest but need nurturing. Regular follow-ups recommended.

COLD

Low Priority

New leads or unresponsive prospects. Occasional follow-ups to maintain contact.

Pro Tips & Best Practices

Effective Follow-up Strategies

  • Be Consistent: Set regular follow-up intervals and stick to them
  • Personalize Messages: Use the {name} placeholder in templates
  • Update Status Regularly: Keep prospect status current
  • Set Realistic Dates: Don't overpromise follow-up timing

App Usage Tips

  • Enable Notifications: Allow app notifications for reminders
  • Backup Templates: Keep important templates saved
  • Review Daily: Check your prospect list each morning
  • Clean Up Regularly: Remove inactive or closed prospects

Troubleshooting

Not receiving notifications?

Check your device settings and ensure the app has notification permissions. Also verify that the app is not in battery optimization mode.

App crashes on startup?

Try clearing the app cache and restarting. If the issue persists, contact support with your device details.

Missing follow-ups after restart?

The app automatically handles device restarts. If notifications are missing, check that the app has the necessary permissions.
