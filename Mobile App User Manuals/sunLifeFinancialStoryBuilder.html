play_circle How to Use the App

  1. Enter Your Information

    Input your current age (18-100 years), gross annual income, and number of dependents. The app validates all inputs to ensure accurate calculations.

  2. Generate Your Financial Story

    Tap the "Generate My Financial Story" button. The app will process your information and create a personalized financial analysis.

  3. Review Your Results

    View your comprehensive financial scenario, including detailed gap analysis and visual comparisons between protected and unprotected scenarios.

  4. Understand the Insights

    Read the key insights to understand how insurance can bridge your financial gap and protect your family's future.

functions Understanding the Calculations

Income Replacement Calculation

Calculates the total income needed until retirement age (65), accounting for inflation:

Annual Living Expenses = Annual Income × 70%
Total = Sum of inflated expenses for each year until retirement
Inflation Rate = 3% annually

Education Fund Calculation

Estimates total education costs for all dependents:

Per Dependent = Sum of 4 years education costs
Total Education = Number of Dependents × Education per dependent
Education Cost per Year = PHP 600,000 (adjusted for inflation)

Financial Gap Calculation

Total protection needed to maintain your family's lifestyle:

Total Gap = Income Replacement + Education Fund + Emergency Fund + Dependent Buffer
Emergency Fund = 6 months of expenses
Dependent Buffer = PHP 3,000,000 per dependent
