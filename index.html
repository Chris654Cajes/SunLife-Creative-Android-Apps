Welcome to SunLife Mobile Apps

Welcome to the comprehensive SunLife mobile application suite designed specifically for financial professionals. These three powerful Android applications help you manage client relationships, analyze rejection scenarios, and conduct financial planning surveys - all in one unified system.

Client Tactics - Rejection Categories

Lack of Trust or Credibility
Client doesn't trust advisor or has past negative experiences
Price / Affordability
Premiums seem too expensive or value perception is low
Lack of Perceived Need
Client feels too young, healthy, or already covered
Poor Timing
Financial constraints or competing priorities
Information Overload
Too many options or complex terms cause confusion
Lack of Urgency
No immediate trigger or perceived consequences
Emotional Resistance
Discomfort discussing mortality or commitments
Comparison Shopping
Client evaluating multiple advisors or offers
Product Mismatch
Plan doesn't fit client needs or budget

Financial Planning - Survey Features

Survey Form Sections

  • Personal Information - Name, email, contact number
  • Family Status - Marital status, number of children
  • Financial Goals - Retirement, education, emergency funds
  • Financial Status - Existing investments, insurance knowledge
  • Savings Location - Where client keeps savings

Management Features

  • Advanced filtering by name, email, marital status
  • CSV export for data analysis
  • Secure local data storage
  • Mobile optimized interface

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • Minimum 50MB storage space
  • Internet connection for initial setup
  • Screen resolution: 720p or higher recommended
